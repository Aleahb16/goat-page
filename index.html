†Jesus Christ†

Who is Jesus?

Jesus is a second chance. He is the Son of God, the Messiah, the Savior of man. He is love, peace, forgiveness and a million
other wonderful things. He is the perfect role model for how we should be as human beings and serves as a constant reminder
that each and every single one of us is loved enough for his ultimate sacrifice; his crucifixion to save us from eternal death.

What is he great at?

Simply everything! Jesus was human, but he was the only perfect one.
Like a human he faced troubles, hardships, and temptations, but being the Son of God,
embodied with the Holy Spirit, he was able to overcome sin and sacrifice himself for the
rest of us who could not. During his time on earth, he healed, he taught, he fed, and most
importantly he saved. Some of the things he did was heal people with leprosy, gave sight
to the blind, cast demons out of the possessed, turned the lost to the Lord, brought peace
to the troubled, and made himself known as The Savior. He was nothing but the perfect
human example of the Lord's everlasting love for mankind.
